Manufacturing method

01


1. In a non stick pan with a little olive oil, fry the tofu until both sides turn yellow. Before frying tofu, it's best to suck water with a dry towel;
2. Take out the tofu, put some olive oil in the pot, fry the onion and fat cow slightly, and then take out the beef.
3. Make sauce. Mix 100ml Japanese soy sauce, 100ml Weilin, 2 tablespoons sake.
4. Add water directly to the pan where the beef has been fried, add the difficult kelp, radish, cabbage, mushroom, add the sauce, cook over medium heat for 10 minutes, and then add the Artemisia rod, cabbage, konjac silk, Flammulina velutipes, tea tree mushroom and tofu. Continue to cook for more than 10 minutes. Only about 1 / 3 of the water is left. Add the fried fat beef slices and onions and cook for a few minutes.

Manufacturing method

02


Soup bottom: 1 / 2 cup of kunbu firewood fish soup, 1 / 2 cup of dessert wine and thick soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar, a little wine. Methods: 1. Wash spring chrysanthemum and Chinese cabbage and cut them into appropriate sizes. Cut the scallions into sections. Wash mushrooms. Scald the medicinal bents with boiling water for a moment and take them out. Ge vermicelli softened in water.
2. Heat in a flat iron pan and turn to low heat. Coat with cream. Add shredded burdock, shredded onion and scallion and stir fry for a while.
3. Add the soup base material, and then add other materials to cook until cooked. Then remove it and dip it in the whole egg juice.
Dipping sauce: whole egg juice
